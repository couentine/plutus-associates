China International Scientific Instrument and Laboratory Equipment

The 15th China International Scientific Instrument and Laboratory Equipment


China International Scientific Instrument and Laboratory Equipment Exhibition (CISILE). sponsored by Instrument Manufacturers Association(CIMA), and supported by China Machinery Industry Federation; China Association for Instrumental Analysis; China Educational Instrument& Equipment Association is Asia’s top scientific show in China. The 15th CISILE 2017 will be held on April 68, 2017 at the China National Convention Center in Beijing, the capital of China.With atotal floor area more than 22,000㎡, exhibiting newly developed analytical and testing instrumentation, optical instrumentation,laboratory equipment, measuring instrumentation, specialized instrumentation and chemical reagents from all over the world.Meanwhile, technical seminars and business talks will be held simultaneously. CISILE 2016 had 563 exhibitors demonstrating their latest technologies from 20 countries.In addition, group pavilions Germany,Taiwan also exhibited their most advanced equipment and technologies. More international companies will join us in the following year.
