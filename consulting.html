Consulting Services

Our last Client

The Medicis Home


The Medicis Home is a luxury senior living complex located in Burgundy, France. With our help, we created a hotel division in their business and promoted this new product to boost revenue. After one year of work the hotel portion became the largest revenue generating part of the business. The hotel division was profitable, booking rates increased by 25% over the year and we helped them build such a strong reputation in service that they won a Booking award in 2015. By partnering together in marketing, management and organizational strategy, Plutus Associates significantly contributed to the success of Medicis' business and pushed their business forward. The firm now receives high volume of customers and the business continues to be more profitable every year.
