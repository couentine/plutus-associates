China International Scientific Instrument and Laboratory Equipment

The 15th China International Scientific Instrument and Laboratory Equipment


The CIMAE - China International Modern Agricultural Exhibition in Beijing is an international event for the agricultural industry. At this exhibition both national and international exhibitors present the latest techniques, materials and innovations for the industry and take the opportunity to develop new businesses. This exhibition is a communication and information platform in the industry and offers exhibiting companies the opportunity to present to an audience of experts. Visitors can find in depth and comprehensive information about the latest developments, trends, products and services in various fields.

CIMAE 2017 has an exhibition area of 50,000 square meters. It is expected to have more than 1,500 exhibitors and attract over 50,000 visitors. The exhibition will set up eight display areas: “Comprehensive Exhibition”, “Internet and Agriculture”, “Pesticides and Fertilizers”, “Agricultural Aviation”, “Protected Agriculture”, “Agricultural Machinery”, “Seeds and Seedlings” and “Soil Prevention and Control”. CIMAE will also organize high profile concurrent activities which include conferences and forums of various professions. The exhibition will build an optimal platform for commercial and trade cooperation of modern agricultural equipment and technology, resource connection, and consultation and communications for the exhibitors, which will power the modernization of China’s agriculture.
